What is Cyber Safety?

Cyber safety refers to the responsible and secure use of the internet, digital devices, and online services. It focuses on protecting personal information, devices, and networks from cyber crimes such as hacking, identity theft, phishing, and online scams.

Why is Cyber Safety Important?

In today’s digital world, people use the internet for communication, education, banking, shopping, and entertainment. Without proper cyber safety practices, sensitive information such as passwords, bank details, and personal data can be stolen or misused.

Cyber safety helps individuals and organizations stay protected from financial loss, privacy invasion, and cyberbullying.

Common Areas Where Cyber Safety is Needed

  • Social media platforms
  • Online banking and shopping
  • Email and messaging apps
  • Online gaming and streaming
  • School and work-related online tools

Who Should Practice Cyber Safety?

Cyber safety is important for everyone — students, parents, teachers, professionals, and senior citizens. Anyone who uses the internet or digital devices should be aware of cyber safety practices.
