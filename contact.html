Need Help?

If you are facing cyber crime, online harassment, fraud, or identity theft, reach out using the options below. Acting quickly can help reduce damage.

  • Email Support:
    cybersafety@help.com
  • Cyber Crime Helpline:
    1930 / 1940 / 999 (Available 24/7)
  • Local Law Enforcement:
    Visit or contact your nearest police station for serious incidents.

Send Us a Message

You can also contact us directly for guidance or general questions.
